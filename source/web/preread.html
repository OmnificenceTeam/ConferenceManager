ADVANTAGES BENEFITS

INTERACTION

  • Opportunity for the organizers to interact with attendees prior to, during and/or after the event
  • Easy for organizers to share conference updates, changes in schedule and/or any comments
  • Provide event details instantly to the attendees at any time

COMPATIBILITY

  • Compatible with iOS, Android or Windows device making it easy for participants
  • Hybrid build framework ensuring low installation package and high flexibility in instant content updation

MULTI-PURPOSE

  • Flexibility to support parallel events (simultaneous polls, Q&A and other interactivities)

TRACKING

  • Collect feedback and user behavior to help understand each attendee’s most and least preferred content

FOR ATTENDEES

  • Ease of navigation, networking, interaction and participation
  • Make use of attendees’ smartphones, avoiding the hassle of providing rented devices or printed materials
  • Increased participation with continuous activity on the app

FOR ORGANIZERS

  • Cost effective and environment friendly, avoiding paper, printing, and rentals
  • Potential reduction in operational costs such as shipping of invites, multiple doctor visits, onsite printing, loss or damage of rented device, etc.
  • Paves the way for access to new customers using social networks and viral communications
  • Map customer behaviour using attendees’ app-usage report on in-app interaction, sections visited, polls, and queries raised
  • Increase the percentage of evaluations returned
  • Increase the level of engagement between peers and attendees-speakers through Q&A, live polls, survey, and feedbacks
  • Extends existing event assets and functions such as registration and logistics

OTHERS

  • Improves and simplifies information distribution
  • Supports more efficient data collection
  • Accommodates the younger generation of attendees
